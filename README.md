### 🧭 About Me

Since 2025, I'm a bachelor student in the program  
<strong>"Intelligent Machines and Equipment in the Agro-Industrial Complex"</strong>  
at the <strong>Timiryazev Agricultural Academy</strong> (Moscow, Russia).

This program combines <em>machine learning</em>, <em>computer vision</em>, <em>automation</em>, and <em>robotics</em> applied to intelligent systems and machines for the agro-industrial sector. My focus is to bring high-tech efficiency to physical production, including logistics, diagnostics, and processing.
